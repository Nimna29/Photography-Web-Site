An Eye for Detail

Composition is Key but the knack for capturing the little details is just as Important. These can take a photo of an ordinary thing, “Go the extra Mile”. The Lighting, Composition, Subject, the Cohesiveness of all these aspects is needed to create the perfect photograph.

Patience and Flexibility

Perfect photographs aren’t created in a hurry. There are many variables that can affect the quality of the photograph at any time. Even when the conditions are perfect a hurried shot can throw off the quality of the photo. It takes patience and dedication to make sure all those things are in Order and to achieve the best outcome. Even when none of the conditions are favorable a good photographer makes the best with what they have.

Good People Skills

Unless you are planning on strictly being a lone nature photographer, the art of communication is essential for the Field. Clients, Other Photographers, Oblivious bystanders, unruly babies are some of the problems that face the everyday photographer. And know-how to connect and communicate with them cannot be understated. A relaxed and focused subject can be the difference between a good photo and one that would be deleted on the spot.
